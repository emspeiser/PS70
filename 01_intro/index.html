For my final project, I'd like to design a ski binding that minimizes ACL tears. Many commercially available bindings only release horizontally at the toepiece (ie, forces applied to the side of the front of the boot will eject the boot) and vertically at the heelpiece. A twisting motion at the heelpiece is one of the main mechanisms for an ACL injury, but is not protected in the traditional ski binding. Another idea I had for my final project ----
